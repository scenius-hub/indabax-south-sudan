About Us

IndabaX South Sudan is part of the continental Deep Learning Indaba movement, which aims to strengthen artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) across Africa. As a local chapter, IndabaX South Sudan serves as a platform to bring together researchers, students, developers, and enthusiasts who are passionate about advancing AI in our context.

The 2025 edition marks the first time IndabaX is being organized in South Sudan, making it a historic step toward building the nation’s AI and tech ecosystem. Through engaging talks, practical workshops, poster presentations, and AI challenges, IndabaX South Sudan is building a community of innovators who are applying AI to solve local challenges and contribute to global knowledge.

This event provides a unique opportunity to learn, connect, and collaborate — empowering South Sudan’s tech ecosystem to play a leading role in shaping Africa’s digital future.
