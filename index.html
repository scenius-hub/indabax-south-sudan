About Us

IndabaX South Sudan is part of the continental Deep Learning Indaba movement, which aims to strengthen artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) across Africa. As a local chapter, IndabaX South Sudan serves as a platform to bring together researchers, students, developers, and enthusiasts who are passionate about advancing AI in our context.

The 2025 edition marks the first time IndabaX is being organized in South Sudan, making it a historic step toward building the nation’s AI and tech ecosystem. Through engaging talks, practical workshops, poster presentations, and AI challenges, IndabaX South Sudan is building a community of innovators who are applying AI to solve local challenges and contribute to global knowledge.

This event provides a unique opportunity to learn, connect, and collaborate empowering South Sudan’s tech ecosystem to play a leading role in shaping Africa’s digital future.

What to Expect

  • Keynote Presentations: Hear from leading African and international experts on AI, data science, and emerging technologies.
  • Workshops & Training: Build skills in machine learning, deep learning, and responsible AI practices through hands-on sessions.
  • Hackathon: Develop creative solutions using real datasets covering weather prediction, disease outbreak detection, and sentiment analysis of social media discussions in South Sudan.
  • Poster Presentations: Researchers and students will present their work in AI and ML, sparking dialogue and knowledge exchange.
  • Exhibitions: Organizations and startups will showcase AI-powered products, tools, and services driving change in our communities.
  • Networking: Connect with peers, mentors, and potential collaborators who share a vision of using technology for sustainable development.

Why Attend?

  • Learn from experts and practitioners in the AI field.
  • Gain visibility for your projects and research.
  • Collaborate on innovative ideas that address real-world challenges.
  • Be part of the movement putting South Sudan on the map of AI development in Africa.

How to Participate:

You can join in several ways:

✅ Attend as a participant to learn and network.

✅ Present a poster or research paper to share your findings.

✅ Exhibit your work to highlight innovations.

✅ Register for the hackathon and build practical AI solutions.

We are excited to welcome students, professionals, entrepreneurs, and community leaders to this groundbreaking event and together shape the future of AI in South Sudan.
