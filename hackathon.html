Hackathon @ IndabaX South Sudan 2025

Join our AI Hackathon to collaborate, innovate, and compete in solving real-world challenges using artificial intelligence for social good.

Overview

This year’s IndabaX South Sudan Hackathon invites developers, data scientists, students, and creatives to build impactful AI solutions aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Theme

“AI for Sustainable Impact in South Sudan” — Solve problems in areas like health, education, agriculture, peacebuilding, and more using AI and data-driven solutions.

Tracks

  • HealthTech & Reproductive Health
  • Climate & Agriculture
  • Digital Literacy & EdTech
  • Peacebuilding & Inclusion

Who Can Participate?

Everyone is welcome — from beginners to experienced developers! You can participate solo or in teams of up to 5 people.

Prizes

Top teams will win exciting tech prizes and mentorship opportunities. Winning ideas may also be incubated further through Scenius Hub programs.
