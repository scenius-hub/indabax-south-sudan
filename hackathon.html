IndabaX South Sudan Hackathon 2025

Collaborate. Code. Create. Shape the future of AI in South Sudan.

About the Hackathon

The IndabaX South Sudan Hackathon 2025 is a high-energy, hands-on event designed to bring together young innovators, developers, students, and professionals to solve local challenges using artificial intelligence and digital technologies. Whether you're new to coding or experienced in machine learning, this hackathon is for you!

Who Can Participate?

  • University students (technical and non-technical)
  • Early-career professionals in tech
  • Anyone with a passion for problem-solving and innovation

Tracks & Challenges

Participants can choose from several exciting themes:

  • Health & Wellbeing
  • Agriculture & Food Security
  • Climate Action
  • Education & Access to Learning

Prizes & Recognition

Top teams will be awarded prizes, mentorship opportunities, and certificates of recognition. Winners may also be invited to present their projects at future tech events.
